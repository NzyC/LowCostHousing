Sydney Airport's Proposed Badgerys Creek Development"

Sydney Airport has announced a delay in their proposed development plan for a new airport at Badgerys Creek, located 50 kilometers west of downtown. Initially expected in the first quarter of the year, the formal "notice of intention" will now be issued in the second quarter after the government has reviewed public feedback on the environmental impact statement and secured funding for the necessary earthworks, which are estimated to cost around $1.5 billion.
Following the issuance of the notice, Sydney Airport will have between four to nine months to determine whether to exercise its option to develop the new airport. If they do, the estimated cost of constructing the runway and terminal will be approximately $2.5 billion. The new airport is projected to open by 2025, featuring a single runway and no curfew, and will be comparable in size to Adelaide Airport.
