About Low Cost Housing

Low Cost Housing offers affordable housing solutions in Australia, interest-free. Founders Ahmad Quesada, Waseem Ghulam, and Sayed Azam bring decades of experience in civil engineering, IT, and entrepreneurship to the table. Ahmad's experience on high-profile projects like Sydney's Kingsfordsmith International Airport and the Olympics Dome informed the company's unique approach to delivering quality homes.

With research and development to perfect their business structure, Low Cost Housing offers a range of affordable housing options, making the dream of home ownership a reality
